Tic Tac Toe

How to Play

Tic-tac-toe is a simple game played by two players, X and O, on a 3x3 grid. Players take turns placing their symbols (X or O) in empty cells of the grid. The goal is to get three of your symbols in a row, either horizontally, vertically, or diagonally.

To make a move, simply click on an empty cell. The game will automatically switch between X and O after each move. The game ends when one player gets three symbols in a row or when the grid is full, resulting in a draw. An alert will appear announcing the winner or a draw, and the board will be reset for a new game.
